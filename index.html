about us

welcome to zion mico finance

ZION MICRO FINANCE (MCS) LTD,was formed in january 2021 to meet financial needs of its members
who are at the grassroot of the ecomomic activities. it is good to note that the environmental scanning conducted in Anambra state in the year 2020 by DR. PETER AMANDI OKAFOR
showed that the low income earners find it difficult to borrow financial facility from banks and people around them. therefore, no strong economic activities to boost profits and consequent savings for futher investment.
therefore, ZION MICRO FINANCE (MCS) LTD, was formed and registered in anambra state with registration number AN 16702 to fill up the lacuna. the company was originally formed by ten subscribers who decided to pull their resources together under given and acceptable conditions in order to run the organization effectively.
the company has the president, vice president, secretary and treasurer as the policy-making body. in addition,the managing director, managers, heads of operations and financial canvassers/ marketers formed a strong structure that runs the business affairs on daily basis.
the firm is designed to provide other essential services/products to different classes of economic groups. some of such products are: savings and loan facilities, business visa procurement, feasibility study, project management, data analysis, resource persons, bank statement reconciliation using CBN guidelines, property acquisition.
